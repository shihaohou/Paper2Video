  - Title: "Gradient Purification"
  - Alignment loss L_align = D_TKL(zero-shot || fine-tuned)
  - Orthogonal projection rule: when <g_task, g_align> < 0, subtract the conflicting component; otherwise keep g_task.
  - One short caption: "Updates that contradict balanced knowledge are projected away."

Slide 6 — Method II: Residual Learning.
  - Title: "Residual Learning in Logit Space"
  - Global branch is frozen and provides balanced baseline logits l_G.
  - Personalized branch learns client-specific parameters phi_k producing l_P^k.
  - Final prediction: l_final^k = l_G + l_P^k (additive fusion in output space).
  - One short caption: "Personalize as additive correction, not feature fusion — bias does not propagate."
